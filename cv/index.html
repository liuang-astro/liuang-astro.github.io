I am a postdoc researcher at Max Planck Institute for Extraterrestrial Physics. I got my PhD in Astrophysics at Sapienza University of Rome in 2019. From 2016 to 2020 I worked at INAF-Arcetri Astrophysical Observatory as a PhD student (2016-2019) and a postdoc fellow (2019-2020). From 2015 to 2016 I was a PhD student at the University of Oklahoma (I quit this PhD program after passing the qualifying exam in Astrophysics). Before that, I was at Beijing Normal University for 7 years (2008-2015) to get the BSc and MSc degrees in Astronomy and Astrophysics. My research mainly involves X-ray observations of galaxy clusters and the related astrophysics and cosmology.
